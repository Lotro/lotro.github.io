Introduction back to ToC

The LOTRO ontology is a project aimed at representing and making available for everyone our work of research and the knowledge that we gained. As a matter of fact, ontologies are «useful tools for sharing and exchanging knowledge, ontologies are a formal, explicit specification of a shared conceptualization».
